About MentorMindz

AI-Powered Mentorship. Built by Youth. Designed for You.

🎯 Our Mission

MentorMindz exists to empower students and learners through AI-based mentorship that is fast, free, personalized, and available 24/7. We believe that education is a basic right, not a luxury.

🌍 Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted AI-powered learning companion — giving every student in every corner of the world a chance to succeed through intelligent, human-like mentorship.

👨‍💻 The Founder: Sreepathi Ravikumar

MentorMindz was founded in 2025 by Sreepathi Ravikumar, a student and tech enthusiast with a dream to democratize education using artificial intelligence. As a learner himself, he experienced the struggles of not finding timely, personalized support — and decided to build a solution that changes that for every learner.

He leads the platform as its Founder & CEO, with the long-term dream of building the world’s most accessible learning engine — starting from India, scaling to the world.

💡 Our Core Values

  • Access for All: Education should be free and fair.
  • Human-Centered AI: Tech should serve people, not replace them.
  • Youth Innovation: Built by students, for students.
  • Purpose Over Profit: We focus on impact, not just revenue.

🚀 What’s Next?

We’re just getting started. With your support, MentorMindz will soon expand into mobile apps, voice-enabled tutors, video classes, and more. Join the movement — the future of learning is already here.
